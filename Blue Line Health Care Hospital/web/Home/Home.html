About us

Our Story

Blue Line Health Care Hospital is an ultra-modern multi-specialty 150 bedded corporate hospital located within the ‘Hospital Zone’ of Blue line street, Colombo 02. We are committed to providing compassionate and high-quality medical care of international standards with a focus on value based healthcare. We also provide the best clinical talent of the country and supplemented it with a highly skilled and competent nursing and technical team, robust support services, a modern infrastructure,the latest medical equipment and diagnostics, with cutting edge technology.

High Quality Patient Comfort and Convenience

When a patient is admitted, the environment in which they stay during treatment or recovery is as important as the medical care they receive. A patient’s recovery benefits from a calm and quiet environment and a high degree of comfort that can ease their pain and anxiety. Blue Line Health Care Hospital’s experienced and sensitivity-trained staff is committed to making patients feel at home by taking care of all their needs.
